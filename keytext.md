# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Scan any book barcode and instantly know if you already own it. No account needed, works offline, zero duplicates.

## Description
Stop buying books you already own. ShelfCheck is the fastest way to prevent duplicate book purchases -- just scan a barcode and get instant feedback.

Ever stood in a bookstore wondering if you already have that book at home? ShelfCheck gives you the answer in seconds. Point your camera at any ISBN barcode and see an instant red, amber, or green result:

- RED: You own this book -- put it back
- AMBER: You might own a different edition -- double check
- GREEN: New to your shelf -- go ahead and buy

KEY FEATURES

Instant Duplicate Detection
Scan any ISBN barcode (EAN-13/EAN-8) and get immediate visual feedback. No waiting, no typing, no searching. Just scan and know.

100% Offline and Private
No account required. No cloud dependency. Your library data stays on your device. Works perfectly in airplane mode -- even in bookstores with no signal.

Continuous Scan Mode (Pro)
Power through your entire bookshelf in seconds. Scan multiple books back-to-back without touching the screen. Perfect for cataloging a large collection.

Smart Book Metadata
Automatically fetches book titles, authors, publishers, and cover images from Open Library and Google Books when online. Works offline with ISBN-only lookup.

Lending Tracker (Pro)
Track who borrowed your books and when. Never lose track of a lent book again.

Custom Tags (Pro)
Organize your library with custom tags. Group books by genre, location, series, or any system you prefer.

CSV Export (Pro)
Export your entire library to CSV for backup or analysis in spreadsheet apps.

My Shelf Library View
Browse, search, and sort your complete book collection. Filter by tags, search by title, author, or ISBN.

Manual Book Entry
Add books without barcodes by entering the ISBN or filling in details manually.

Torch Control
Toggle the flashlight for scanning in dim bookstores or library stacks.

FREE vs PRO

The free version lets you scan and track up to 50 books with full duplicate detection. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited books, continuous scan, lending tracker, custom tags, and CSV export.
